## CodersSecret SEO interpretation

Use Google docs as the source of truth. For CodersSecret, prioritize:

1. Helpful learning content over search manipulation.
2. Crawlable, server-rendered/static critical content.
3. Clean metadata and canonical URLs.
4. Accurate structured data only.
5. Stable internal links and URLs.
6. Fast, mobile-friendly, accessible page experience.
7. No spam, doorway pages, hidden keywords, fake schema, or thin AI-generated content.
